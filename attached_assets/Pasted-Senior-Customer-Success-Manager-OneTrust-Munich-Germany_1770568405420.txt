At a Glance
As a Senior Customer Success Manager at OneTrust, you will act as a trusted advisor for strategic customers, ensuring their success with the AI-Ready Governance Platform. The role focuses on customer satisfaction and product utilization, making it essential for clients navigating data governance and compliance. This position is pivotal in shaping the future of responsible data use in businesses worldwide.

About the Company
OneTrust’s mission is to enable innovation through the responsible use of data and AI. They developed the first technology platform for responsible data use in 2016 and are focused on unifying regulatory intelligence, automation, and governance workflows.

Responsibilities
Establish relationships with customer stakeholders and executive sponsors.
Act as the primary contact to help customers navigate OneTrust.
Accelerate customer solutions through knowledge of their business.
Deliver proactive communication and manage mission-critical escalations.
Align customer’s roadmap with OneTrust’s product roadmap.
Advocate for customers by sharing their key business requirements internally.
Understand each customer's industry and Trust management strategy.
Deliver business reviews to communicate with customers and their executive teams.
Utilize adoption metrics to share best practices and identify growth opportunities.
Manage an account portfolio to balance retention and growth.
Deliver and communicate ROI for customers throughout their lifecycle.
Minimum Qualifications
BA/BS in a relevant subject.
Around 5 years in a client-facing role within CSM, professional services, or technical consulting.
Effective customer-facing communication skills.
Management of Enterprise, Strategic customers.
Ability to build relationships with key customer stakeholders at all levels.
Preferred Qualifications
Track record of successful engagement across corporate functions.
Strong entrepreneurial skills to excel in a complex environment.
Experience implementing SaaS solutions.
Experience using SFDC, Gainsight, and other CS Technology applications.
An ambitious person who takes the initiative to get things done.
Skills Required
German language proficiency.
Customer success management.
Technical aptitude.
Proficiency in Salesforce (SFDC) and Gainsight.
