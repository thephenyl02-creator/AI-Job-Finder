Our Story

From Legal Data Infrastructure to AI Transformation
Founded in 2022
273 Ventures set out to build the next generation of legal data infrastructure. We built the technology, trained the models, published the research, and open-sourced what the industry needed most. That work gave us an unmatched understanding of how AI actually works in legal and professional services — and what it takes to get it right.

Built the Foundation
Created the Kelvin Agentic OS and trained KL3M, the world's first Fairly Trained legal language model.

Open-Sourced for the Public Good
In 2024, donated open-source data and model assets to the ALEA Institute, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit we founded to steward these public goods.

Published the Frameworks
Authored "Agentic AI in Law and Finance" as an open textbook, establishing the GPA framework now used across the industry.

Advising the Industry
Today, 273 Ventures is the premier AI transformation partner for law firms, legal departments, legal service providers, and legal technology companies.

Intellectual Ecosystem

Research, Publications & Open Source
Built on Published Research
Our advisory and training work is grounded in frameworks we developed and published openly. When we advise on AI strategy, we draw on research cited hundreds of times and tested across dozens of organizations. This is not generic consulting — it is guidance rooted in peer-reviewed, publicly available work.

Agentic AI in Law and Finance
Open textbook, CC BY 4.0, available on SSRN and Amazon. Establishes the GPA (Goal, Perception, Action) framework for understanding agentic AI systems.
