As a Manager of the Deal Desk at Legora, you will drive the development of innovative legal solutions in collaboration with legal professionals. The role requires a strong understanding of legal workflows and the ability to integrate AI technology effectively. This position is exciting for those passionate about redefining legal processes and working in a fast-paced, transformative environment.

ABOUT US Legora is on a mission: to redefine how legal work gets done. From the very start we have been very clear about the fact that we are not building a solution for lawyers, we are building it with them, because it is the only way to make sure it gets done the right way; working side-by-side every step of the way.

Our AI-native workspace empowers legal professionals not just to work faster - but to ask better questions, unlock new insights. Every day, we push the boundaries of legal tech to make complex processes smarter, faster, and more human.

From thousands of documents analysed in minutes to intelligent workflows designed in collaboration with leading practices, we’re turning possibility into reality.

Today we are trusted by global firms like Cleary Gottlieb, Goodwin, Bird & Bird and Linklaters in over 40 countries, but we have no plans on stopping here.

We ship fast, we iterate effectively, and we scale rapidly - not by accident, but by design. When you join Legora, you become part of a team that believes "good enough" isn’t good enough and that the way to win is together, by empowering lawyers to do their best work with technology that truly understands them.

If you’re excited by building from first principles, working with exceptional people, and accelerating change in a high-stakes, high-impact domain—then this is the moment and the place.

WE’RE NOT JUST SHAPING THE FUTURE OF LEGAL TECH — WE’RE DEFINING IT. READY TO JOIN US IN BUILDING THE INTELLIGENT FUTURE OF LAW? We’re hiring a Manager, Deal Desk to build and own how deals are structured, reviewed, and executed globally at Legora.

This is a foundational role with a clear mandate: enable sales velocity across regions while protecting pricing integrity, policy compliance, and revenue accuracy at scale.

You’ll be a strategic partner to Sales leadership and a central node between Sales, Finance, Legal, and GTM Operations worldwide. This role blends strategic judgment with hands-on execution - ideal for someone who enjoys building global, scalable processes while staying close to the details that make deals work.

As Legora continues to scale across regions, products, and business models, you’ll play a key role in shaping how we sell, how we book revenue, and how we maintain operational excellence globally.

WHAT YOU’LL DO You’ll take full ownership of the global Deal Desk function, serving as the primary point of contact for deal review, structuring, and approval across regions.

You’ll work closely with Sales leadership to ensure deals align with Legora’s commercial strategy while meeting internal policy and revenue standards. More than that, we believe you will thrive by taking ownership of: - Owning and managing the global Deal Desk function, partnering closely with Sales leadership to structure, price, and approve deals accurately and strategically - Positioning Deal Desk as a trusted advisor earlier in the sales cycle, helping accelerate deal velocity and improve deal quality across regions - Reviewing deals for policy alignment, compliance, and booking accuracy, including pricing, configuration, approvals, and escalation of non-standard terms - Partnering cross-functionally with Legal, Finance, Billing, Revenue, Collections, and GTM Operations to streamline global quote-to-cash workflows - Driving operational improvements by identifying process gaps, implementing scalable solutions, and maturing Deal Desk systems as the business grows - Supporting Finance during month-end close and audit cycles, ensuring deals are correctly reflected across systems globally - Analysing deal trends and process efficiency across regions, using data to surface insights and drive continuous improvement - Enabling and educating Sales teams globally on policies, processes, and best practices to improve compliance and speed - Representing Finance in cross-functional initiatives tied to global selling motions, product launches, and business model evolution - Acting as a builder in a fast-growing environment, proactively developing frameworks, tools, and processes that support global scale WHO YOU ARE You’re an experienced Deal Desk leader who combines strong commercial instincts with operational rigour.

You’re comfortable operating at the intersection of Sales and Finance globally, and you know how to balance speed with control across regions and time zones.

More than that, we believe you bring: - 7+ years of experience in Deal Desk or related commercial operations roles, with experience owning global or multi-region scope - Proven ability to build strong relationships with Sales leadership and cross-functional partners across geographies - Experience supporting multiple business models, including subscription-based and usage- or consumption-based revenue - Strong execution skills and the ability to manage competing priorities in a fast-paced, high-growth, global environment - A builder mindset - comfortable working through ambiguity, maturing processes, and implementing scalable improvements - Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, using data to inform decisions and improve outcomes - Experience managing high deal volumes and tight timelines during end-of-month and end-of-quarter cycles - Clear, concise communication skills, with the ability to influence stakeholders and advocate for Deal Desk as a strategic partner Nice to have: While not required, this role can be an especially strong fit if you’ve helped scale Deal Desk or commercial operations across multiple regions or time zones.

WHY LEGORA This is a rare opportunity to build and lead Legora’s global Deal Desk function from the ground up. You’ll have real ownership, direct influence on how we sell worldwide, and a meaningful role in shaping the systems and processes that support Legora’s growth at scale.

More than that, we offer: - Competitive compensation and meaningful equity - A high-performance, high-trust environment with low ego - The chance to build something foundational with global impact Legora is an Equal Opportunity Employer At Legora, we believe great teams are built on diversity of thought and experience.
